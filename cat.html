Breeds

Popular cat breeds

Persian

Known for long fur and a calm, affectionate nature. They need regular grooming and a peaceful environment.

Maine Coon

Large, friendly and playful. Maine Coons are social cats who enjoy human company and interactive toys.

Siamese

Vocal, intelligent and very affectionate. Siamese cats thrive on attention and mental stimulation.
